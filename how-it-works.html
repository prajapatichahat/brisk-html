Trust Architecture

A Governance Layer

BRISK isn't just a noticeboard. We govern each transaction to eliminate common friction points.

Vetting & Verification

Every trader undergoes background checks, license verification, and reference validation prior to active bidding.

Secured Communications

Chats are saved on the platform, providing clear documentation of agreed terms and minimizing disputes.

Protected Negotiations

Mutually approved milestones govern releases, so traders know they will get paid and customers know quality will be met.

Receive status notifications, uploaded photos of milestones, and direct task updates directly through the app.
