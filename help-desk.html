How BRISK Works

BRISK simplifies local services into a transparent 6-step flow. Customers post job requirements with photos and timeline, while verified local traders send tailored offers and negotiate scope seamlessly within the platform.

Trader Verification

Trust starts with verified credentials. Every trade professional on BRISK undergoes identity verification, business licensing checks, and insurance audits before bidding on customer jobs.

Smart Milestones

Break complex jobs into clear, manageable milestone deliverables. Funds are agreed upfront and released only when work phases are completed and verified by the customer.

Escrow Payment Security

Protect your capital with BRISK Escrow. Customers know their money is safe until work meets agreed standards, and traders get guaranteed payout certainty upon job completion.

Dispute Resolution

Our dedicated resolution desk steps in whenever scope or quality disagreements arise. With complete audit trails of messages and milestones, fair resolutions are reached quickly.

Trader Growth Hub

Expand your business with automated lead matching, instant client messaging, electronic invoicing, and performance analytics to build a top-rated reputation in your local community.
