Why We Exist

The Local Services Challenge

For decades, local services have remained deeply fragmented. Finding a reliable service provider felt like a lottery, while pricing structures were opaque and agreements were handshakes with zero security.

BRISK was built to resolve this friction. By standardizing the communication flow, verifying participant identities, and recording terms of engagement on a platform-governed database, we replace uncertainty with trust.

"We are restructuring the trade landscape, putting transparency at the core of every connection."

— The BRISK Team

Core Values

Our Founding Principles

These core philosophies govern how we build, iterate, and run the BRISK network daily.

Trust

We believe safety is not optional. Every piece of user identity, chat log, and feedback loop is protected to secure trust.

Transparency

Scope, milestones, quotes, and deadlines are transparently recorded, eliminating surprise costs or delayed scopes.

Verification

We perform active credentials checks, ensuring our trader pool represents authentic local industry professionals.

Fair Negotiation

No rigid pricing mechanisms. Traders bid, customers review, and scope is discussed directly in-app.

Community Growth

We empower local services sectors to digitize their operations, receive verified credit, and expand their client circles directly through structured feedback and reviews.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the BRISK platform, trust architecture, and services.

BRISK is a trust-first, negotiation-driven digital marketplace connecting customers and local service providers. Unlike simple noticeboards, we govern each transaction to ensure pricing is fair, terms are documented, and both parties are protected.

Every trader on BRISK undergoes background checks, license verification, reference audits, and insurance validations by our dedicated team before they are allowed to submit quotes or bid on projects.

Posting jobs and receiving quotes is 100% free for customers. For traders, we charge a small platform commission fee only when contracts are successfully won and signed off.

All client-trader negotiations, price agreements, and milestone checkpoints are recorded in-app, establishing a platform-secured single source of truth. Structured completion sign-offs ensure traders get paid on time and customers get the quality they agreed upon.

You can find verified professionals across a variety of home improvement and service sectors, including plumbing, electrical installations, painting, full renovations, landscaping, and masonry.
